Our projects & Events

We do Sanitary drives, programs for first time employees to get jobs, women summits, books

"We raise awareness of the of the 2030 Agenda and align with SDGs 1, 3, 5, 6, 8, 10 via our projects. Sanitary Towel Bank initiative This initiative is helping to reduce period poverty in Africa by hosting Sanitary product drives at learning insitutes, women shelters and under privileged communities. The 'Pathways to Dignity' program was launched to normalize periods and break the taboos surrounding menstrual health by education both genders across different generations. Breaking cycles of generational poverty Parents have a powerful impact on the relationship that children and youth have with wealth creation. The #WFN foundation program has been implemented to rewire limiting beliefs and education children and youth on best to position themselves in the economy. Accelerate 4.0 level 1 Mentorship program Aimed at first time job seekers and graduates. There is no cost to join this program. Accelerate 4.0 Level 2 Mentorship program is $150
